## Overview
Ongoing career development, independent of any single interview cycle: leadership growth, story bank, resume, reading, portfolio project, and the career journal. This is an Area (standard to maintain), not a Project — it doesn't end when interviews end.

## Sub-areas
- [[Leadership/]] — leadership notes, mentorship, decisions, EM/staff-track thinking
- [[Behavioral Stories/]] — reusable STAR stories, tagged by theme, mapped to companies in [[Interview Prep 2026]]
- [[Resume/]] — resume versions per role archetype
- [[Reading Notes/]] — engineering blogs, papers, books (template: `Reading Notes`)
- [[Portfolio Project/]] — the side project used to demonstrate staff-level judgment
- [[Career Journal/]] — dated reflections, not tied to a project
