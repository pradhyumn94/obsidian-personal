# Interview Notes

## What belongs here
- `Resume.md` — your working resume content and versioning notes.
- `Behavioral Stories.md` — your STAR story bank.
- `Company Notes/` — one note per company you're interviewing with.

## How to use it
- Keep `Behavioral Stories.md` as a bank of reusable stories; link to specific stories from a company note rather than rewriting them per company.
- In `Company Notes/`, track what you know about the company, the loop structure, and how your prep maps to what they're likely to ask.
- Update `Resume.md` as you gain new wins during this 6-month cycle — don't let it go stale until the week before you start applying.

## Naming convention
- `Company Notes/` files are named exactly after the company: `Stripe.md`, `Google.md`.
- `Resume.md` and `Behavioral Stories.md` are fixed names — don't rename them.
