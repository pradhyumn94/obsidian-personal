## Goals
- DSA: fluent recall of all core patterns, no "reset to Day 1" by month 4
- System design: 15+ designs drafted and reviewed, comfortable in 45-min unstructured format
- Distributed systems + backend fundamentals: staff-level depth, not just definitions
- AI platform engineering: able to design a production agentic system end-to-end under interview pressure
- Behavioral: 12-15 STAR stories covering ownership, conflict, failure, influence, technical leadership
- 2+ mock interviews per week by month 3
